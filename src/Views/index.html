Flash Sale Engine — Documentation & Tutorial

Part 1: Overview & Usage

What is this? This is a high-throughput Distributed Order Matching Engine. It is engineered to process thousands of concurrent trading requests (such as high-frequency trading or flash sales) and instantly match buyers and sellers with sub-millisecond latency using an entirely in-memory architecture.

How to use the dashboard:

  • Step 1: Navigate to the "Authentication" panel below, create an account (Sign Up), and log in to generate your secure JWT Token.
  • Step 2: Paste your JWT Token into the "Place Order" form above.
  • Step 3: Enter a Quantity and Price, then click the BUY or SELL button.
  • Step 4: Your order will instantly appear in the Order Book. When a BUY order's price meets or exceeds a SELL order's price, a Trade is executed and broadcasted to the "Live Trade Feed".
